About Us

Newmoon Crafts is an embroidery company in Kenya that was founded in December 2020 with a dream to bring beauty at home through bead work.

We are a team of young creative, professionals skilled in weaving shambalas and household items e.g cutlery holder, serviet holder and spoon holders and and tablemats

Our Technical skills and experience are grounded on the different challenges that the customer brings forth. We believe that every challenge is an opportunity to learn and scale higher.
